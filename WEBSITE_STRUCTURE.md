## Design & Visual Direction

**Color Palette Ideas:**
- Primary: Deep blue (trust, tech, industrial)
- Accent: Bright cyan/teal (action, intelligence, data flow)
- Neutral: Dark grays, white
- Warning/Alert: Amber (for downtime/risk indicators)

**Visual Style:**
- Clean, technical, professional
- Data visualization elements (not just decorative)
- Industrial imagery (plants, equipment) but elevated/modern
- Abstract representations of data flow ‚Üí insights ‚Üí action
- Before/after comparisons (reactive vs. proactive)

**Typography:**
- Modern sans-serif for headings (geometric, clean)
- Readable serif or sans for body text
- Monospace for technical/code elements

**Key Visual Metaphors:**
1. **The 4-layer stack** (Sensors ‚Üí Cloud ‚Üí Analytics ‚Üí Actions) - this should be a consistent visual throughout
2. **Data flowing into decisions** - animated or illustrated journey
3. **Human + AI collaboration** - not replacing people, augmenting them
4. **Before/After** - chaos/reaction vs. control/prevention

## Content Tone & Voice

**Tone:**
- Confident but not arrogant
- Technical but accessible
- Problem-focused, then solution-focused
- No buzzwords without substance
- Evidence-based (cite BCG, industry stats)

**Avoid:**
- Generic "AI will solve everything" language
- Overpromising ("eliminate all downtime")
- Jargon without explanation
- Feature-dumping without context

**Embrace:**
- Specific numbers (2-5% improvement, 60-90 days)
- Real questions plants ask
- Honest about what's required (data access, SME time)
- "We don't need perfect data" messaging
- Fast ROI framing
